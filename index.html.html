About KQ Education ERP

__

To reduce the manual workload of these administrative activities, Frontedge Data Solutions Limited introduced KQ Education ERP, a specialized system that manages all activities and processes that go into the running of an Academic Body

An Academic body may be an educational system, a regulatory system or schools at every level. The system makes it possible for every part of the institution or College to function smoothly and interact with one another or with entities outside their systems seamlessly and as required. The system comprises of the biometric time and attendance, Human Resource Management, Students Information Management, Computer Based Testing, Transcript Management, and lots more The ERP Software is delivered as a service, which will be securely cloud hosted. As a result, it will always be available online for use wherever it is needed and all you will need to access any of its modules is an internet connection. In this model, all updates are automatic, in that the College using it has no worry about keeping things up to date as we will handle it all on our end. Alternatively, it can be self-hosted by a College, whereby after selecting the required modules online, they can be bundled, downloaded and set up within the College easily. The solution also possesses a unique functionality that allows integration with pre-existing solution(s) that might already be adopted by the College. ERP Features
